Who We Are

Arodos Technologies started as a homecoming — when our founder returned from Bengaluru to Assam, driven by the dream of building something meaningful with and for his people.

What began as one person’s mission has grown into a passionate team united by purpose — to create world-class tech solutions that empower others. Together, we craft ERP systems, AI tools, and custom apps that help businesses, startups, and institutions thrive.

Today, our journey is shared with clients across India, the US, and Canada. At every step, we believe in building not just software, but long-term partnerships rooted in collaboration, trust, and shared success.

Our Vision

At Arodos, innovation drives us forward. We explore bold ideas and technologies, crafting impactful solutions that evolve with client needs, ensuring we always stay ahead in a fast-changing digital world.

Our Mission
